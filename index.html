About me

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at UIUC with a dual degree in Computer Science, Statistics, and Mathematics. I have been very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hari Sundaram. It has been a great honor and pleasure to join his research group. I have also worked closely with Prof. Chengxiang Zhai and Prof. Hanghang Tong, who have given me a lot of help and support.

My current research focuses on User Behavior Modeling in the context of Social Networks. I also hold a broad interest in the domains of Data Mining, Machine Learning, and Causal Inference.
