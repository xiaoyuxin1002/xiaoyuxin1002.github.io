About me

I am a master student at CMU, majoring in Machine Learning. I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science, Statistics, and Mathematics at UIUC where I was fortunately advised by Prof. Hari Sundaram, Prof. Jiawei Han, and Prof. Chengxiang Zhai.

My current research focuses on using machine learning with graphs to better understand social interactions. Broadly speaking, I am interested in the intersection of Machine Learning, Data Mining, and Computational Social Science.
